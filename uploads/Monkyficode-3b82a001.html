Terms of Service Agreement

Rev: 2026.1

1. Scope of Work

Monkyfi provides professional consulting, optimization advice, and software integration services specifically for telecommunications platforms. All engagements will proceed under mutually executed Statements of Work (SOW).

2. Project Duration

Standard active project engagements run from one (1) week up to several months depending on deployment scale and optimization parameters specified in the custom timeline.

3. Deliverables

All formal deliverables are provided in the form of detailed structured project documentation, design schematics, deployment scripts, or configured dashboards as outlined in individual SOW agreements.

4. Client Responsibilities

Clients must assign a dedicated point of contact, coordinate access to necessary data structures, networks, or cloud platforms, and provide accurate system performance metrics.
